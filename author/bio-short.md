## 100-Word Bio

> For back cover, Amazon author page, and conference programs.

Saurav Bhatia is the Founder and CEO of Yirifi.ai, a 20-person AI regulatory intelligence startup where just two people built 15 backoffice microsites in three months. With 20+ years in banking and fintech, he served as interim CEO of Singapore's first digital bank TRUST (1M+ clients, ~20% market share) and founding CFO of Mox Bank in Hong Kong (750K clients, 12% market share). Previously he held leadership roles at Standard Chartered Bank and Citibank across Singapore and Hong Kong. He holds an MBA from NUS and completed the Oxford Blockchain Strategy Programme. He writes at blog.sauravbhatia.com.
