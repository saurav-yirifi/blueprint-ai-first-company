02 — About the Book

Most companies bolt AI onto existing workflows and call it transformation. This book argues that the real shift requires rethinking the entire operating model — how you build products, structure teams, manage data, and make decisions.

Across four parts and twelve chapters, Blueprint for an AI-First Company covers the strategic mindset shifts, infrastructure patterns, agent architectures, team structures, data strategies, and governance frameworks that separate companies using AI from companies built on it.

Every framework comes from real experience. At Yirifi.ai, a 20-person AI regulatory intelligence startup, just two people built 15 backoffice microsites in three months — the book documents what worked, what failed, and what the patterns look like when AI is genuinely the foundation.
